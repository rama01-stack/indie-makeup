About Us

Indie MakeUP is an inclusive, eco-conscious makeup studio created to celebrate every face and every shade. We believe beauty is a form of self-expression, not a standard to fit into. Our mission is to offer makeup that is kind to your skin and gentle on the planet, using cruelty-free and sustainable products whenever possible. From everyday looks to special occasions, we create personalized styles for all genders, skin tones, and identities. At Indie MakeUP, beauty means confidence, creativity, and care — for you and for the world around you.
